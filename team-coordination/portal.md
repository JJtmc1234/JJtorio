# JJtorio

A work in progress overhaul for Factorio 2.0. The goal is an endless,
procedurally generated universe where every planet forces a different way to
build. Inspired by the shape of Space Exploration, never its systems.
Standalone, not compatible with Space Age or Space Exploration.

## Playable today (0.1.x)
- Light, faster early game. More reach, cheaper and faster research, quicker
  tier 1 machines, and a bigger starting inventory.
- The rocket silo is earned. Its research is gated behind a mature base, the
  full science line, electric furnaces, bots, express belts, and nuclear.

## Planned for 0.2 and beyond (NOT in the game yet)
- Endless procedurally generated planets, each with its own class, terrain,
  resources, day length, and gravity.
- Reach orbit, a real space surface, and build an original orbital science tier.
- Planets that each force a different way to build, a real constraint like heat,
  gravity, or storms that breaks blueprint copy paste. This is the core idea.
- An early game restructured so vanilla blueprints do not just paste in.
- A new extraction mechanic, core mining caliber, tied to each planet's traits,
  plus custom biome and space tiles.

## Compatibility
Standalone. Not compatible with the Space Age expansion or Space Exploration.
Targets Factorio 2.0.

## Status
Early work in progress by a young solo modder, with a little help. 0.1.x is the
foundation and early game. The space content above is the 0.2 goal, not yet
playable.
