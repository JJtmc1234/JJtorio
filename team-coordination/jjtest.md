# jjtest

Quick checks, about 30 seconds each. Mark done, or error: <what>, or skip.
Tip: use /editor to jump straight to items and surfaces, no need to play up to them.

1. load: enable JJtorio, start a new base 2.0 save, no red error.
2. /jjt-new-planet then /jjt-goto <name>: teleports you onto a planet.
3. /jjt-orbit: drops you on a platform that reads as space.
4. glance at planet ground per class: frozen pale, volcanic dark, barren sand, rocky grey.
5. press T: the science chain shows after the rocket silo, rocket through stellar.
6. click the rocket-silo tech: it lists the mature base prereqs.
7. in /editor, search the inventory for the new items: assembling machine 4, turbo
   transport belt, turbo inserter, exotic circuit, exotic rounds magazine.
