Version: 0.1.27
Date: 2026-07-22
  Features:
    - 24 trigger techs. These research themselves when you hit a milestone like
      crafting 2000 belts or building 200 assemblers, no science needed, each
      granting a small bonus. They reward scaling the factory up.
    - New late game gear. A Heavy Tank, a Heavy Turret with more range, Exo Armor
      with a bigger equipment grid, an Exo Shield, and Exo Legs. Cloned from base
      gear and buffed, unlocked by three new techs.
  Bugfixes:
    - Painted planets no longer keep Nauvis grass decoratives, rocks, and cliffs
      on top. Trees are kept so fertile stays lush.
  Changes:
    - Oceanic is now a sea with sand islands. Fertile paints green ground.
    - The exotic magazine hits harder, double round damage on top of the double
      magazine size.
