# JJtorio

A work in progress space overhaul for Factorio 2.0. Endless procedurally
generated worlds, a light early game, and the depth in space. Standalone, not
compatible with Space Age or Space Exploration.

The four planning docs live at the root.
brainstorm.md is the idea and the why.
planning.md is the plan, milestones, and versioning.
infrastructure.md is how it is built.
progress-report.md is the live status.

Coordination and design notes live in team-coordination/. The shippable mod is
in mod/, the dev scripts are in tools/.

Community feedback is welcome. Email jjtmcmultiversal@gmail.com with the subject
JJtorio.
