# Brainstorm

How we arrived at JJtorio.

## The idea
JJ wanted a major Factorio overhaul that is fun and fairly complex. We chose a
space overhaul in the shape of Space Exploration rather than Space Age. That
means an endless, procedurally generated universe instead of a small set of
handmade planets.

## Key choices
1. The early game stays close to vanilla but lighter and faster. The depth
   lives later, in space.
2. Keep the vanilla rocket silo as the way to reach space, but gate its
   research behind a mature base.
3. Space is a navigable tiled surface. After the silo you reach orbit and make
   a new orbital science.
4. Every planet forces a different way to build, so blueprints do not just copy
   paste from world to world.

## Originality rule
Space Exploration inspires the shape, never the systems. We avoid its four
specialized sciences, its piloted spaceships, and its data and energy beaming.
We invent our own mechanics, like a Core Tap that keys off each planet's rolled
facts. You plan self running colonies, you do not pilot a ship.

## Compatibility
Standalone. Not compatible with Space Age or Space Exploration. Targets
Factorio 2.0.
